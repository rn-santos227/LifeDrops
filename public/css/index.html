Kubling Tago Resort is owned by Mrs. Edwina L. Suarez, opened on the 8 of February 2012. It is located at Villa Maria 2 Subdivision Cupang Mayamot Antipolo Rizal and it is managing now by her daughter Jenna Suarez. “Kubling Tago” it means hidden, the name of resort speaks for itself because the location of the resort is far from city and it build in a subdivision, you will have thought that there is no resort in the area unless you discover it in catalogs and fliers or by nearby residents in the area. Kubling Tago Resort is very peaceful place to be in because it has a nice facilities and clean area, it is fit for those who wants a relaxing and stress free environment.
